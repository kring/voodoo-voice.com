Personalities define the sounds or voices that Voodoo Voice uses to respond to your voice commands. You can think of a Voodoo Voice personality as a theme or a skin. The difference is that it stylizes your audio experience instead of your visual experience.
Installing a Personality
Installing a Voodoo Voice personality is easy. Simply download the ZIP file and unzip it anywhere on your system. Then, load Voodoo Voice and click the "Open New Personality" button on the toolbar. Select the file with the PSN3 file extension from the ZIP file you downloaded and extracted. Voodoo Voice will load the new personality and tell you when it is ready to accept voice commands.
